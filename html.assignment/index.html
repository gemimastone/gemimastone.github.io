My Goal

My dream/goal in life is to be able to own my own private clinic so that I can use the skills I have gained and the passion I have for the Industry into that cause. To support that dream, I reached out to my first passion which is Art; Becoming a UX/UI designer will help me by having a double income to help fund my private clinic. The Beauty Industry is expensive equipments wise, therefore it is good to have another source of income while starting out. Something that I think would be important for you to know about me is that I describe myself as someone that wants to explore every talent that I have and grow with it to see where it can take me. Every day that I live is one step closer to my destination. I always want more and more, just because I do not want to do the same thing for the rest of my life as there is so much to discover and do in life.
